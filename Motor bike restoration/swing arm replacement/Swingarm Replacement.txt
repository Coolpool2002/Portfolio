Replaced the swingarm on a 2021 Royal Enfield Himalayan due to wear and seized chain adjusters.

    Removed rear wheel, suspension linkage, and pivot spindle

    Installed new swingarm with fresh bearings and anti-seize on all hardware

    Replaced sacrificial spindle nut and chain adjuster blocks

    Verified chain alignment and torqued all fasteners to spec

    Improved long-term serviceability and rear-end stability

This project showed practical mechanical skills, torque spec awareness, and methodical part replacement.
