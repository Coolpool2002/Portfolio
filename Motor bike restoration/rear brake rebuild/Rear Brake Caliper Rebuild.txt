Rebuilt the rear brake caliper on a 2021 Royal Enfield Himalayan after piston seizure.

    Removed the caliper and disassembled the piston assembly

    Cleaned corrosion and debris from piston and bore

    Replaced seals and re-lubricated components with brake-safe grease

    Bled the brake system and verified full piston movement

    Restored proper brake function and pedal feel

This project demonstrated hands-on experience with hydraulic systems, precision cleaning, and brake maintenance.
