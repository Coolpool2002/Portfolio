Hayden Jones – Hardware & Systems Portfolio

Welcome to my project portfolio. I'm a hands-on tech enthusiast with a passion for designing, modifying, and building systems from the ground up. My work spans electronics diagnostics, embedded systems, 3D design, and practical repair skills across a wide range of tools and platforms.

A large part of my workflow involves 3D printing and rapid prototyping. I use an Ender 3 V3 SE printer to bring designs to life, producing strong, functional parts in materials such as PLA, TPU, PETG, and ABS. From structural brackets to flexible mounts, I tune prints for strength, precision, and durability depending on the use case.

I regularly work with embedded systems like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, integrating them with custom circuitry and sensors to build smart devices, mods, or automated tools. Projects often include soldering, breadboarding, and crafting enclosures in CAD software like FreeCAD or Fusion 360.

This site showcases a collection of my technical builds, practical experiments, and engineered solutions—each focused on learning, improving, and solving real problems through hands-on work.
